Delete Your Tukani Account

Developer: Open Loops

You can permanently delete your Tukani account and its associated learning data.

Delete inside the app

  1. Sign in to Tukani.
  2. Open Account options.
  3. Select Delete account.
  4. Confirm Delete permanently.

The in-app option deletes the account immediately after confirmation.

Request deletion without the app

If you cannot access Tukani, send a deletion request from the email address used for your Tukani account. Include the words “Tukani account deletion” in the subject. We may ask you to verify ownership before processing the request.

Request account deletion

Data deleted

Account deletion removes your Tukani account, authentication sessions, course plans, assigned lessons, progress, answers, scores, completion history, streaks, study days, Google or Apple account identifiers stored by Tukani, and other learning data associated with the account.

Data that may be retained

Tukani does not retain the deleted learning profile for normal app use. Limited records may be retained only when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or dispute-resolution obligations, and only for the necessary retention period.
